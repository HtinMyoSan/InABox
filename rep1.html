Sesame Chicken with Lo Mein & Green Beans


Ingredients

  • 1 pkg (1 lb) Wegmans Chicken Breast Strips for Stir-Fry
  • 3 Tbsp water
  • 4 Tbsp Wegmans Pure Olive Oil, divided
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Wegmans Cornstarch
  • 1 pkg (12 oz) Wegmans Cleaned & Cut Trimmed Green Beans
  • 1 pkg (21.15 oz) Wegmans Lo Mein Noodles (Frozen Foods)
  • 2 cloves Wegmans Peeled Garlic, minced
  • 1 pkg (12 oz) Wegmans Cleaned & Cut Asian Slaw
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup Wegmans Organic Sesame Garlic Sauce
  • 1 Tbsp white roasted sesame seeds
Get Recipe

Directions

  1. Place chicken in small bowl. Add water, 1 Tbsp at a time, working in with hands until water is absorbed. Drizzle 1 Tbsp of oil over chicken and work in with hands until absorbed. Sprinkle chicken with cornstarch; work in with hands to coat. Cover; chill 30 min.
  2. Blanch green beans in large pot of boiling, salted water 3-4 min. Remove beans with slotted spoon; drain and set aside. Add noodles to blanching water; blanch 1-2 min. Drain and set aside.
  3. Drizzle 1 Tbsp oil around sides of stir-fry pan; tilt pan to coat evenly. Heat on HIGH until oil faintly smokes. Add half the chicken; sear meat on one side until browned, about 3 min. Sear other side. Transfer to clean dish. Wipe pan. Add 1 Tbsp oil to pan on HIGH; repeat process with remaining chicken. Remove chicken from pan; set aside (do not wipe out pan).
  4. Drizzle remaining oil around sides of pan; tilt pan to distribute evenly. Heat on HIGH until oil faintly smokes. Add garlic, Asian slaw, and green beans. Stir and toss, 2-3 min. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add chicken, noodles, and sesame garlic sauce to pan. Stir 2-3 min to heat through. Garnish with sesame seeds.
