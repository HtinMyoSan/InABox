Chicken & Ramen Noodle Soup


Ingredients

  • 1 container (32 oz) Wegmans Organic Chicken Bone Broth
  • 1/3 of a 9.5 oz pkg Wegmans Organic Ramen Wheat Noodles
  • 1 pkg (16 oz) Wegmans Just Picked Far East Stir-Fry (Frozen Foods)
  • 1 pkg (about 1 lb) Wegmans Unseasoned Chicken Breast Strips
  • 4 Tbsp Wegmans Red Miso Paste
Get Recipe

Directions

  1. Add broth to stockpot; bring to boil on HIGH.
  2. Add noodles; cook, stirring occasionally, 4 min.
  3. Add vegetables; stir to combine. Return to simmer. Reduce heat to MED-HIGH.
  4. Add chicken; stir to combine. Return to simmer. Cook 6-7 min until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Stir in miso paste; cook, stirring, until heated through.
