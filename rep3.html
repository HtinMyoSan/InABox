Slow-Cooked Thai Red Curry Chicken


Ingredients

  • 1 can (4 oz) red curry paste
  • 1 can (13.5 oz) Wegmans Organic Unsweetened Coconut Milk
  • 1 pkg (about 1 3/4 lbs) Wegmans Organic Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Wegmans Organic Red Bell Pepper, cored, seeded, 3/4-inch dice
  • 1 pkg (6 oz) Wegmans Petite Baby Bella Mushrooms, washed, quartered
  • 1 white onion, peeled, thinly sliced (about 2 cups)
  • 2 Tbsp Wegmans Pan Searing Flour
  • 1/2 bunch Wegmans Fresh Basil leaves, divided
  • Juice of 1/2 lime (about 1 Tbsp), plus 1/2 lime cut in wedges, for garnish
Get Recipe

Directions

  1. Add paste, coconut milk and chicken to 6-8 qt slow cooker; stir to combine.
  2. Cover; cook on HIGH 3 hours or LOW 6 hours.
  3. Add peppers, mushrooms, and onions. Stir in pan-searing flour.
  4. Cover; cook on HIGH 45 min. Add 10 basil leaves; stir until wilted. Stir in lime juice and chicken. Garnish with remaining basil leaves and lime wedges.
